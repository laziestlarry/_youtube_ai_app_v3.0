Client Onboarding QA Checklist

Readiness basics:
- Primary contact and champion identified
- Access to required systems provided
- Success metrics agreed

Discovery questions:
- Top 3 processes causing friction?
- Current SLAs and pain points?
- Compliance or data residency constraints?
- Existing automation tools in place?

Go/No-Go before automation:
- Policies available (support, refunds, data)?
- Test data/sandbox ready?
- Escalation owners confirmed?
