Example Larry Flows (outline)

1) Customer FAQ Handling
- Trigger: new support ticket with common intents.
- Actions: classify intent, draft reply with policy snippets, request missing info.
- Human loop: agent approves drafts over refund threshold or sensitive topics.
- Outputs: send reply/update ticket, log satisfaction prompt.

2) Internal Status Summary
- Trigger: daily/weekly schedule.
- Actions: pull key metrics, summarize top risks/issues per dimension.
- Human loop: ops lead reviews before distribution.
- Outputs: email/Slack digest, update Notion dashboard cards.

3) Risk & Incident Notification
- Trigger: incident label or high-risk flag in tickets/monitoring.
- Actions: gather context, draft incident note, propose mitigation steps.
- Human loop: incident manager approves communications.
- Outputs: incident channel update, creates follow-up tasks.
