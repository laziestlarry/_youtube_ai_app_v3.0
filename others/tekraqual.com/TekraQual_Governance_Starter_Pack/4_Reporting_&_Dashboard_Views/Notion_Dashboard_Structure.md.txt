# Notion Dashboard Structure

Databases:
- Initiatives (Status, Owner, Dimension, Due date, Impact, Effort)
- Risks (Impact, Likelihood, Owner, Mitigation, Status)
- Metrics (Name, Source, Cadence, Target, Current)

Views:
- Board by Dimension (OPS/CX/GOV/DATA/INCOME)
- Timeline for Initiatives
- Risk register table filtered by Open status
- Metrics list with sparklines/targets

Linked pages:
- Readiness results snapshot
- Meeting notes + decisions log
