Larry CoPilot Workflow Design Canvas

Fields to capture per workflow:
- Inputs: sources (tickets, forms, CRM events, email inboxes)
- Steps: what Larry does (classify, summarize, draft, route, update tools)
- Human approvals: thresholds, roles, escalation rules
- Outputs: updates to systems, notifications, reports
- KPIs: response time, quality, automation %, error rates
- Guardrails: data masking, limits, forbidden actions
