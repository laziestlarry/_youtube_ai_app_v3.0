# Revenue Streams Workflow

Purpose: standardize the path from lead to delivery for every revenue stream.

## Standard Stages
1) Signal: inbound lead, market signal, or product demand.
2) Qualification: fit, budget, and readiness check.
3) Offer: proposal, pricing, and scope.
4) Activation: contract, onboarding, and kickoff.
5) Delivery: execution and reporting.
6) Retention: ongoing value and expansion.

## Revenue Streams

### YouTube Automation
Inputs
- Content briefs, niche research, and assets
KPIs
- Cost per video, time to publish, RPM, retention rate
Workflow
- Brief -> script -> voice -> edit -> publish -> analytics

### SaaS Subscriptions
Inputs
- Product roadmap, onboarding flow, support coverage
KPIs
- MRR, churn, activation, LTV
Workflow
- Signup -> activation -> usage -> retention -> expansion

### Consulting and Advisory
Inputs
- Discovery, scope, and KPI targets
KPIs
- Win rate, delivery cycle time, margin
Workflow
- Discovery -> proposal -> kickoff -> delivery -> renewal

### Digital Products
Inputs
- Templates, playbooks, or toolkits
KPIs
- Conversion rate, refund rate, review score
Workflow
- Build -> package -> launch -> optimize

### Marketplace and Affiliate
Inputs
- Listings, offers, and partnerships
KPIs
- Click-through, conversion, payout rate
Workflow
- List -> optimize -> promote -> measure

## Revenue Operations Checklist
- One owner per stream.
- Weekly pipeline review with blockers.
- Monthly expansion plan per stream.
