# Organization Design (Revenue Operations)

This structure balances growth, delivery, and automation.

## Core Pods

### 1) Growth Pod
- Mission: acquisition + conversion
- Roles: growth lead, content strategist, ad ops, lifecycle email
- KPIs: CAC, conversion rate, lead velocity

### 2) Commerce Pod
- Mission: product catalog + marketplace listings
- Roles: product manager, listing specialist, catalog ops
- KPIs: AOV, conversion rate, listing velocity

### 3) Fulfillment Pod
- Mission: delivery + support
- Roles: delivery lead, support coordinator, QA
- KPIs: delivery time, satisfaction, refund rate

### 4) Automation Pod
- Mission: workflows + integrations
- Roles: automation engineer, backend lead, data ops
- KPIs: automation coverage, failure rate, cost per order

### 5) Brand Pod
- Mission: brand system + creative direction
- Roles: brand lead, designer, copy lead
- KPIs: brand consistency, content quality, NPS

## Executive Layer
- Strategy lead (portfolio direction)
- Operations lead (delivery and reporting)
- Finance lead (revenue and margin control)

## Weekly Cadence
- Monday: pipeline + backlog
- Wednesday: launch review
- Friday: revenue review + optimizations
