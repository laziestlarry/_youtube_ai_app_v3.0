# Community Post Templates (Reddit + Discord)

## Reddit (value-first)

### Post 1: Hybrid passive income stack
Title: "I built a passive income stack in 2 hours/week — here's the workflow"
Body:
1) Short context: hybrid schedule + limited time.
2) Breakdown: asset → listing → automation → tracking.
3) Share 1 screenshot or checklist.
4) Ask: "What part of the workflow is hardest for you?"

### Post 2: Automation SOPs
Title: "SOPs that removed 10+ hours of weekly admin"
Body:
1) List 3 SOP areas (delivery, customer support, updates).
2) Offer a free checklist or snippet.
3) Invite feedback on improvements.

## Discord (community engagement)

### Announcement: new resource drop
- "New drop: Hybrid Passive Income Stack"
- 3 bullet highlights.
- CTA: "Reply with your current goal for a custom tip."

### Weekly prompt
- "What is your 1 automation win this week?"
- "Share 1 dashboard screenshot or a KPI you hit."

## Blog cross-post (summary)
- Title: "The hybrid passive income system that doesn't burn you out"
- 3 subheaders: Asset, Automation, Distribution
- CTA to Shopify/Gumroad listing
