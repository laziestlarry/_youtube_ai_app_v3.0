# Omni-Channel Growth Plan (Hybrid Millennials)

## Audience snapshot
- Age 27-42, hybrid work, time-poor, income-curious.
- Wants side income without full-time commitment.
- Values simple systems, clear ROI, and fast wins.

## Core positioning (passive income hooks)
- "Build once, sell repeatedly" digital assets.
- "Automation-first" workflows that reduce daily effort.
- "Skill stacking" for stable side income (content + ops + templates).

## Channel execution

### Shopify (primary storefront)
- Focus on bundles and higher AOV packages.
- Use tiers: Starter (templates), Pro (automation kits), Elite (consulting).
- Upsell: checklist + onboarding call + reporting pack.

### Etsy (discovery + evergreen)
- Emphasize design-led digital bundles and practical templates.
- Lead with "instant download" and "workspace friendly" tags.
- Test 3-5 listing variants per SKU for keyword coverage.

### Amazon (scale + authority)
- Convert top bundles into guides or workbooks for KDP.
- Offer companion downloads via QR to Shopify.
- Build a brand storefront and bundle series volumes.

## Social media (organic + paid)
- TikTok/Reels: 15-30 sec hooks, "I made $X with 3 templates."
- YouTube Shorts: before/after dashboards, system walkthroughs.
- LinkedIn: case study posts for hybrid professionals.
- Paid: retarget site visitors with "bundle + starter kit" offers.

## Email marketing (7-day launch sequence)
1) Day 0: "Your first automated income asset."
2) Day 1: "3 assets that sell while you work."
3) Day 2: "Template stack: build your first listing."
4) Day 4: "How hybrid pros sell in under 90 minutes/week."
5) Day 5: "Customer story + ROI breakdown."
6) Day 6: "FAQ + friction removal."
7) Day 7: "Last call + bonus pack."

## Network marketing + partnerships
- Affiliate pack for micro-influencers and coworking communities.
- Partner with productivity newsletters for bundle swaps.
- Offer "hybrid hustle" webinars with revenue-sharing.

## Metrics to track
- Shopify conversion rate, bundle attach rate, email CTR.
- Etsy favorites-to-cart ratio, tag CTR, repeat buyers.
- Amazon KDP page reads and series conversion.
