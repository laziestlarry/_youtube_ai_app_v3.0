# Product Portfolio (Commercialization-Ready)

This portfolio maps existing assets in the repo into sellable products/services and assigns channels, pricing, and fulfillment paths.

## Portfolio Principles
- Lead with digital goods (fast delivery, high margin).
- Offer service tiers that upsell from products.
- Keep SKUs modular so they can be bundled, remixed, and re-listed.
