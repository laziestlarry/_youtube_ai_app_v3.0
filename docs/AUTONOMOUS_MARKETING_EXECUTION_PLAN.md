### 2.3 Platform-Specific Tactics

#### LinkedIn Strategy
```
Daily Actions:
- Comment on 10 relevant posts (add value, not "Great post!")
- Send 5 personalized connection requests to target personas
- Share 1 insight post with specific data/results
- Engage in 2 relevant groups

Weekly Actions:
- Publish 1 long-form article
- Create 1 carousel post (high engagement format)
- Host or participate in 1 LinkedIn Audio/Live
```

#### Twitter/X Strategy
```
Daily Rhythm:
- 7AM: Industry insight or tip
- 12PM: Engagement post (question, poll, opinion)
- 5PM: Behind-the-scenes or progress update
- 9PM: Thread or valuable resource

Weekly:
- 1 mega-thread (10-15 tweets) on specific topic
- 1 Twitter Space hosting/co-hosting
- Engage in 3 relevant hashtag conversations
```

#### YouTube (Organic SEO) Strategy
```
Content Types:
- Tool demos and walkthroughs (bottom-funnel)
- Industry tutorials (top-funnel SEO traffic)
- Case study videos (social proof)
- Shorts repurposing TikTok/Reels content

SEO Optimization:
- Target keywords: "AI automation", "YouTube automation tools"
- Optimize titles, descriptions, tags
- Create playlists for binge-watching
- Add end screens and cards for retention
```
