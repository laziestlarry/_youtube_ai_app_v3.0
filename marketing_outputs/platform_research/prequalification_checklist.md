## QUICK REFERENCE: TIER DEFINITIONS

**Tier 1 - Immediate:** No barriers, can post now
**Tier 2 - Ready:** Low barrier, brief setup needed
**Tier 3 - Research:** Unknown requirements, needs investigation
**Tier 4 - Build:** Karma/reputation building required (1-4 weeks)
**Skip:** Not aligned or too restrictive
